Online Exam Guidelines for Candidate

A. General Instructions -

  1. Attention !! Read all below instructions carefully before starting the exam.
  2. Technical Requirements: Ensure your device meets the technical requirements for the exam platform. This may include a stable internet connection, a webcam, a microphone, or specific software.
  3. Environment: Ensure you are in a quiet and well-lit environment to avoid distractions and ensure webcam clarity if required.
  4. Honesty Policy: Adhere to the honesty policy of your institution. This usually means that all work submitted should be your own, and any form of cheating or plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  5. Start Exam: Enter your name and then read & accept the declaration, which will enable "Start Exam" button.

B. Question Paper -

  1. No. of Questions: There are total 15 questions. Questions visited and total questions will be shown on top right corner of screen.
  2. Time Allotted: Total time is given for this exam is 10 minutes. Timer will appear on top mid-section of screen.
  3. Marks: There is +1 Mark for each correct question and 0 Marks for un-attempted question. There is No negative marks on wrong question.
  4. Score: Total score of candidate will be shown at the end of exam on click of "Finish" button.

C. During the Exam -

  1. Navigation: Navigate through questions using the "Save & Next" button. Avoid using the browser's back button to prevent any loss of data.
  2. Changing Response: Use "Clear Response" button to deselect a chosen answer if you wish to change your response.
  3. Time Management: Be aware of the start time and duration of the exam. Make sure to allocate enough time to complete the exam without interruptions.
  4. Technical Issues: If you encounter any technical issues during the exam, contact your instructor or the technical support team immediately.
  5. Review: Review all answers before submitting, as you may not be able to retake or revise your answers after submission.
  6. Submission: Click "Finish" button to submit your exam. Ensure that you submit your answers before the time limit expires. Your exam will automatically submit your answers when the time is up.
  7. Close Exam: Click on the "Close" button which will appear at the end of your exam, to finish your exam successfully.
  8. Quit Exam: In case of any emergency, there is "Hard Quit" button which will end your exam (This will not give you any score and will end your exam with NO result).
  9. Feedback: After the exam, take the time to review your answers if this feature is available. This can provide valuable feedback for future exams.

Note: Remember, these are just general guidelines. Always check with your instructor or the exam guidelines provided by your institution. Good luck!
